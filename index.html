Know yourself as a reader.

  • How long does it actually take you to finish a book?
  • Which months do you read most? Which hours of the day?
  • How does this year compare to the last?

Lecturameter turns every session into answers. With annual Wrapped, reading bingo and challenges you define.

Timer

Keeps counting with the screen off

Tap Start and read. When you finish you'll see your real pace: pages per minute, total reading time and whether you're getting faster. Functional pages subtract prologues and indexes so the number is honest.

Library

Your full reading history, at a glance

Reading, Read, Rereading, Want to read, Dropped. See how many books you've finished, which genres you gravitate towards and what's still on your list. Automatic covers and Goodreads import.

Statistics

Discover your reading patterns

Do you read more in summer? At night? Which books keep you most consistent? The heatmap and yearly stats reveal it all at once.

🔒
Your data stays only on your device
Lecturameter does not collect or send your data to any server. Your library, sessions, and statistics are stored exclusively on your phone. If you enable Google Drive backup, only you have access to those files. No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking. Read the full policy →

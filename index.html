📚 How Lecturameter works

Real screenshots of the app. Every screen is designed so that logging your reading takes as little effort as possible.

Timer

Keeps counting with the screen off

Tap Start and read. When you stop, the session is saved automatically with its time, pages and pace. Functional pages subtract prologues and indexes so your pace is the real one.

Library

Real shelves

Reading, Read, Rereading, Want to read and Dropped. Automatic covers and Goodreads CSV import.

History & stats

Every session logged

Per-book summary, monthly heatmap and yearly Wrapped with your top authors and genres.
